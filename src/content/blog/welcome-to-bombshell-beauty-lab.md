Bombshell Beauty Lab is the rebrand of Lisa Turley At Salon Studios, built around
a simple promise: feel gorgeous, get noticed.

Every client should leave with a clear plan tailored to *their* hair, *their*
routine, and *their* goals. No assembly line. No pressure. Just thoughtful
guidance and a beauty-lab experience with a little edge.

## Why "by appointment only"?

Because great hair deserves time, attention, and a calm room. Bombshell Beauty Lab
is designed for planned visits, clear recommendations, and client-focused care.

You'll get:

- A real consultation before any decisions are made
- A custom hair plan based on *your* color, shape, texture, and routine
- Time to ask questions: every question
- A team that knows what you are working toward

## Meet Lisa

Lisa Turley began her journey in the hair industry in 1991. In her early years as
a stylist, she became an educator for Rusk, an international haircare company.
That experience shaped her philosophy around balance, natural-looking color,
precision texturizing, and styling education.

- Hair industry experience since 1991
- Former Rusk educator
- Old Town Scottsdale native
- Focus on color, shape, balance, and everyday styling

## What we offer

Precision haircuts, dimensional color, blonding and highlights, and styling
guidance. The service menu should stay focused on Lisa's hair expertise unless
Bombshell Beauty Lab adds additional confirmed services later.
